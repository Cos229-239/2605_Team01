Short Plan:
	Razor Statement:
		- A calendar just like all calendars, with reliability, functionality, accessibility with little flavor.
		- Keeping you organized with 'easy-to-use', intuitive tools, for planning.
		- Designed with you in mind, engaging, easy-to-read calendar.
		- This will become your complete all-in-one planning companion.
	French Vanilla Calendar:
		- Calendar with the work week/7 day week/starts on a selected day
		- Can place a countdown that helps to track important events and color will adjust with importance
		- Can invite/assign others to your calendar and events 
		- Task list
		- Can track birthdates based on day month and year
		- Can share lists for shopping or recipes
		- Dark mode
		- (Possible expansion of AI to help with organization or gift ideas etc.)
